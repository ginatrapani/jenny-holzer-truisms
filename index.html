What is this?

A mini interactive tribute to Jenny Holzer's Truisms made by Gina Trapani.

Sparked by a visit to Holzer's Light Line exhibit at the Guggenheim in New York City in 2024.

Made with hand-written HTML, JavaScript, CSS, Google Fonts, Zed, zertosh/jenny-holzer-truisms, and many Stack Overflow posts, MDN, and W3 docs. No frameworks, builds, AI, or best practices involved.
